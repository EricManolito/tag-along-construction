Home Foundation Site

Building a home in a classic method, includes a solid foundation and set plans for construction. For this container bin home development, the foundation area will be cleared for the container bin dimensions. Estimates will be a large 100' X 100' square space. The house wil be shaped as an L shape with main entrance(s) facing the east. That will be directed to the eastern direction mountains. Eastern direction doorways are essential for Navajo cultural norms.
