The Project

Real-time documentation of building a debt-free, off-grid homestead from raw high desert earth. No shortcuts, no loans — just phased, intentional work.

The land sits between the Nacimiento Mountains to the east and dramatic mesa cliffs to the west, at the edge of the Rio Puerco valley. Purchased in January 2023, it was covered in dense sagebrush and some juniper. Phase 0 clearing began immediately and continues today.

The build centers on an L-shaped container home — six shipping containers, east-facing entrance per Navajo cultural tradition, off-grid solar, rainwater catchment, and a year-round greenhouse.
